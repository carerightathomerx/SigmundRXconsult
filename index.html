About This Practice

Pharmacist expertise, delivered where you need it.

SigmundRXconsult is founded and operated by a licensed registered pharmacist (RPh) based in California, with over 10 years of clinical experience in both inpatient and outpatient pharmacy settings, with deep expertise in transition of care.

A core area of expertise is transition of care — the critical period when patients move from hospital to home, skilled nursing facility (SNF), long-term care hospital (LTCH), or home health. This transition is one of the highest-risk moments for medication errors, and SigmundRXconsult provides professional guidance to patients, families, and caregivers to navigate it safely. If a loved one is hospitalized, get help understanding what they're receiving, what to ask the care team, and how to manage their medications confidently after discharge.

Outpatient expertise spans MTM, chronic disease management (diabetes, hypertension, hyperlipidemia), infectious disease and HIV pharmacotherapy, pregnancy and lactation safety, and travel health — all backed by current evidence and clinical guidelines.
